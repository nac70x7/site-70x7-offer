2. Pricing and Payment

  • Landing Page ($997): Full payment required upfront via Stripe. Work begins after payment is received.

All prices are in USD. Prices are subject to change but will be honored for projects already in progress.

Each tier includes a set number of revision rounds (1–3 depending on plan). A revision round consists of one batch of feedback addressed in a single update. Additional revisions beyond included rounds are billed at $150/hour, quoted before work begins.

  • After 7 days or once a custom domain is connected to your site, the sale is final.
